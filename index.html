How to Play

Getting started with Monster Survivors is straightforward. Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the battlefield. Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies, so focus on positioning and dodging incoming attacks. As you defeat monsters, you'll gain experience points that allow you to level up and choose from various upgrade options.

Strategic thinking is crucial for long-term survival. Consider which upgrades complement your playstyle and current situation. Some upgrades focus on increasing damage output, while others improve defense or mobility. Experiment with different combinations to find the most effective strategies for surviving the increasingly difficult waves of monsters.

Tips for Success

Success in Monster Survivors requires both tactical thinking and quick reflexes. Always keep moving to avoid being overwhelmed by enemy attacks, and try to position yourself to fight smaller groups of enemies rather than large hordes. Pay attention to your health and look for opportunities to create breathing room when your health gets low.

When choosing upgrades, balance offensive and defensive options based on your current needs. Early in the game, focus on damage and attack speed to clear enemies efficiently. As the game progresses and enemies become stronger, consider investing in defensive upgrades and area-of-effect abilities to handle larger groups of monsters effectively.
